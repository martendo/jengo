How To Play

On your turn...

  • Click on a block to select it. It will be highlighted in red.
  • A red square will move up and down on the left. Press the "Stop" button when it is in the shaded region.
  • If you successfully stop the square in the shaded region, the selected block will be removed, and you will be awarded 50 points.
  • If not, your turn ends without having changed the tower.
